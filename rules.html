Instructions

Read all instructions carefully before proceed forward.

• Upon entering the DarkShop, you will be granted 100 Umbra.

• Umbra is the currency used to purchase items from the shop.

• Each item has its own cost. Spend your Umbra wisely.

• Certain items require a sacrifice in addition to Umbra.

• Every Umbra spent and every sacrifice made is permanent.

• Nothing given to the DarkShop can ever be reclaimed.

• Choose carefully before proceeding forward.
